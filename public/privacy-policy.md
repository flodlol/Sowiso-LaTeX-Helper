# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 30, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the **Sowiso LaTeX Helper** Chrome extension.

## What data is collected

The extension does **not** collect personal data, does **not** create user accounts, and does **not** use analytics or tracking.

## How the extension works with your data

- Text you type or paste (for example, LaTeX formulas) is used locally in your browser to generate preview and insert content into Sowiso pages.
- Extension preferences (such as theme settings) are stored locally using `chrome.storage.local`.
- For formula preview rendering, formula content is sent to `https://latex.codecogs.com` to generate preview images.

## Data sharing

The extension does not sell or share personal information. Data is only sent to CodeCogs for preview image generation, as described above.

## Data retention

Stored preferences remain in your browser until you clear extension data or uninstall the extension.

## Security

No dedicated backend server is used by this extension. Processing is primarily local in your browser.

## Children's privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.
